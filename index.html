Biography

Dr. Archana M Nair is an Associate Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering at IIT Guwahati. She has worked as a Scientist at the National Centre for Earth Science Studies and as a Petrophysicist at Shell Technology India. Her research interests span remote sensing, rock physics, hydrology, and critical zone processes.
